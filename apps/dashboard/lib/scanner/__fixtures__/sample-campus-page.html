Main Campus

The Main Campus is the heart of Greenfield University, established in 1965. Spanning 500 hectares of beautiful parkland, it houses our core academic programs, research centers, and student facilities.

Faculties at Main Campus

  • Faculty of Natural Sciences - 6 departments, 32 programmes
  • Faculty of Humanities - 10 departments, 55 programmes

Campus Facilities

  • Central Library with 2 million volumes
  • State-of-the-art research laboratories
  • Student residence accommodating 5,000 students
  • Sports complex and recreation center
  • Medical center and counseling services
